User Roles

Users can have multiple roles on EchoRoots:

  • Contestant: A designer participating in competitions to showcase talent and win prizes.
  • Customer: A shopper interested in artistic, culturally inspired products.
  • Culture Enthusiast: A community member passionate about culture and creative sharing.
  • Potential Creator: A user inspired to eventually become a contestant or artist through community engagement.

User Journey

1) Registration & Login

New users should register an account by providing a mobile number and email address. Once registered, users can log in to access full platform features.

2) Browsing Home (Discover the Platform)

Entry Point:Upon login, users land on the Home page.

On the Home page, users can:

  • See banners for the latest competitions.
  • Explore trending cultural goods.
  • View cultural livestream events.
  • Discover “Top Designers” and “Top-Selling Products”.

Your Choices:

  • Interested in competing? ➔ Tap on the competition banner.
  • Interested in shopping? ➔ Scroll through the trending products.
  • Want to learn about cultural events? ➔ Explore featured livestreams.

3) Joining a Competition (For a Contestant)

Steps:

Step 1: Explore Competition Details

  • Navigate to the Competition tab.
  • Browse the Ongoing Competition promotional poster.
  • Tap "View Details" for full information, including:
    • Theme, submission window, prize pool, participation scope.

Step 2: Sign Up

  • Fill in the Sign-Up form:
    • Author Name, Contact Number, Email Address.
  • After successful signup, the system redirects to the submission step.

Step 3: Submit Your Work

  • On the Submission page:
    • Enter Work Title, Inspiration/Concept.
    • Upload Main Image and Detail Images.
  • Submit the entry by clicking "Submit".

Step 4: Track Your Work

  • Visit View My Work to see submitted entries.
  • Monitor the Submission Date, Competition Ranking, and Details.

4) Voting for Your Favorite Designs (Become a Supporter)

Steps:

  • Go to the Voting section.
  • Browse through submitted entries, each showing:
    • Title, Author, Current Votes, Current Ranking.
  • Click "Vote" for your favorite designs.
  • Already voted items will be shown as "Voted".

Note:

  • Each user can vote once per work.

5) Shopping for Cultural Goods (Become a Customer)

Steps:

Step 1: Browse the Shop

  • Enter the Shop tab.
  • Explore by categories: Hot, New, Paper-Cutting, Dragon Dance, etc.

Step 2: View Product Details

  • Tap on any product to see full information:
    • Materials, craftsmanship, design background, user reviews.

Step 3: Add to Cart or Buy Now

  • Click "Add to Cart" for later checkout.
  • Or click "Buy Now" to proceed immediately.

Step 4: Fill in Order Details

  • On the Fill in the Order page:
    • Confirm Shipping Address.
    • Choose Delivery Method (e.g., Express Transportation).
    • Add any Notes for the seller.

Step 5: Payment

  • Select your preferred Payment Method (Alipay or WeChat Pay).
  • Click "Submit Order" to complete the payment.

6) Joining the Community (Become a Storyteller)

Steps:

Step 1: Explore the Community

  • In the Community tab:
    • See posts under “Recommend” and “Follow”.
    • View trending topics, user posts, and official news.

Step 2: Post an Update

  • Tap "+" (Post) to create a new update.
  • Write your thoughts, upload images, and select hot topics like:
    • #SpecialOffer, #CulturalHeritage, #DragonDance.

Purpose:

  • Share your creative process or product experiences.
  • Promote your submitted work and gather supporters.
  • Engage and build connections with fellow culture lovers.

7) Managing Your Profile

Steps:

  • Access your profile via the Profile tab.
  • View and manage:
    • My Orders, Collection Record, My Designs, Rights Center.
  • Update account settings in Account and Security.
  • Reach out to customer support if needed.

Typical Usage Scenarios

  • Scenario 1 (Designer):
    I am a designer ➔ I see the “Cultural Crafts Competition” ➔ I sign up ➔ I submit my work ➔ I track my ranking ➔ I win and my product goes to market.
  • Scenario 2 (Customer):
    I browse the community ➔ Discover a beautiful Dragon Dance Vase ➔ Read the product story ➔ Place an order ➔ Share a photo review in the Community.
  • Scenario 3 (Culture Enthusiast):
    I love traditional culture ➔ I post about Paper-Cutting Arts ➔ Get likes, comments, and make friends with fellow enthusiasts.
