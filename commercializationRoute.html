Commercialization Path

1) Early Stage: Traffic Accumulation and Influence Building

Core Goal: Attract users, build brand awareness, and lay the foundation for future commercialization.

1.1) Specific Strategies

Design Competitions to Drive Traffic:
Collaborate with art schools, individual artists, and cultural organizations to host low-threshold design competitions, attracting designers, students, and cultural enthusiasts; Offer rewards (such as cash prizes, promotion opportunities, commercialization support, etc.) to encourage participants to publish works and promote them through the platform; Competition themes can involve traditional cultural elements, such as paper-cutting and calligraphy, combined with modern design styles to inspire creativity and interest.

Cultural Content to Enhance User Stickiness:
Release cultural promotional videos (e.g., intangible cultural heritage skills, traditional arts) through short video platforms (Douyin, Xiaohongshu, etc.) to attract traffic; Partner with intangible cultural heritage inheritors for live interactive sessions, spreading cultural knowledge and increasing user engagement; Video content may showcase the process of cultural craftsmanship or stories of traditional techniques' inheritance.

Low Cost Traffic Generation Activities:
Hold flash sales of cultural products, distribute coupons, and run promotions to attract user registrations, encourage interactions, and increase user activity; Collaborate with museums and cultural institutions to plan online visits or virtual exhibitions to enhance brand exposure.

2) Mid Stage: Diversifying Revenue Channels

Core Goal: Explore and expand the platform’s revenue sources and build a diversified profit model.

2.1) Specific Strategies

Monetizing Product Sales: B2B Products: Collaborate with museums and cultural tourism bureaus to launch co-branded cultural products, setting up revenue-sharing models (e.g., platform commissions of 10%15%). B2C Products: Attract individual artists and intangible cultural heritage artisans to join, collecting commissions or entrance fees based on transaction amounts. Custom Demand Services: Offer customized services to collectors or businesses, with the platform taking a commission based on the project value (e.g., 10%20%). Advertising Revenue: Provide homepage ad slots or video stream displays, charging promotional fees to art schools, cultural brands, or museums; Set up advertising packages to attract cultural brands for advertising, boosting platform revenue. Paid ValueAdded Services: Offer designer skill training, work promotion services, and other valueadded functions to help designers increase their exposure and expand their market; Launch VIP membership services, providing exclusive design courses or work showcases.

3) Later Stage: Ecosystem Building and Brand Deepening

Core Goal: Build a closed-loop ecosystem for the platform, enhance brand influence, and foster user loyalty.

3.1) Specific Strategies:

Cultural and Creative Ecosystem Development:
Regularly host design competitions and commercialize the winning works, creating a complete ecosystem from design → production → sales; Limited production of winning competition works to increase market interest and buying desire for platform products.

Launching the "Designer Support Program":
Assist potential designers in transforming their creativity into commercial success, growing alongside the platform; Provide mentorship, financial support, and other resources to help designers build their brands on the platform.

Cultural IP Collaborations:
Collaborate with popular cultural IPs (e.g., TV shows, games, museum exhibitions) to develop co-branded cultural products, increasing product value. For example, collaborate with the Palace Museum or local museums to launch co-branded products or connect with popular movie art elements to attract fan groups.

Community Based Operations:
Build a community for cultural and creative enthusiasts, providing a space for user interaction and work display, fostering word-of-mouth marketing; Launch a user points system to reward active users and offer benefits such as product exchanges and discounts.

International Expansion:
Position the platform as a cultural export window, collaborating with overseas museums and cultural institutions to expand the international user base; Showcase the platform at overseas cultural festivals and exhibitions to attract global users.
