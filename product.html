Platform Introduction

EchoRoots is a comprehensive platform centered around cultural creative design and lifestyle aesthetics, combining three major sections:

  • Creative Competitions
  • Cultural Goods Marketplace
  • Community Interaction

On EchoRoots, users can:

  • Register and participate in themed cultural design competitions.
  • Submit their original designs and compete for rewards and exposure.
  • Browse and purchase curated cultural creative products.
  • Share their creative journeys and cultural experiences with the community.
